A PlayMode is the way in which player input is handled. It is easiest to imagine PlayModes as mini interactive
applications for the player. A play mode could facilitate an in game BBS or note editor, or an AFK handler, or 
virtually any other method of player interaction imaginable. PlayModes are one of the most powerful features of Lucid,
and the sky is the limit as to what is possible.
